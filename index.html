Goals

I want to offer an immersive artistic experience of Nature.
One that not only entertains, but teaches as well.
To cause reflection on our relationship and impact on the world around us.
So if you share a passion for nature, our environment, and video games...
I invite you to join me on this journey!

Concept

This project began with a simple idea. Make a video game that let's people experience nature from a different perspective. A game that allows exploration, learning, crafting, and growing. A way to reflect on our interaction and impact to the environment. All while having fun in an artistic 2D platformer game!
